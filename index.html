Game Jam Games

Several interesting games concepts that some team members and I had played around with in the Global Game Jam over various years.
The source code could be found on the Global Game Jam site.

Box In

Swipe to draw lines across the screen.
Try to box in the moving balls as much as possible.
Red balls bounce off walls.
Yellow balls go through walls.
Random power ups/downs are spawned every level to keep things fresh.

Space Grids: Puzzle

A puzzle game focused on a 7x7 grid in space.
Try to find a route for all the spaceships to reach the destination planet by boosting across stars.
Earn more stars by solving each puzzle with the less moves.

Fortress Fighters

Defend your fortress from waves of enemies and earn gold.
Fortify your castle and build from a large selection of defenders with various skill upgrades, from the Angry Bowman and the Excellent Engineer, to the Ice Wizard and Pyromaniac, and more.
Make your way around the world map and defeat all the bosses.

Bat Dolphin

Fly through the air and swim in the water with this 2D sidescroller where the terrain is invisible.
Use your echo location ability to navigate through the level whilst eating fireflies and fish to earn more points.
